VARIABLES
name description unit
id This is a unique identificator for each downloaded document, the first letter indicates the base which the documents was downloaded from (E for embrapa and S for Scopus). index
author Name of the first author of the document. Full author surname followed by initials of first and middle names. text
publication_year Year of the publication of the document. year according to the gregorian calendar
title The title of the document. text
publication_type The type of the publitation. If the document was published in a peer reviewed journal, or in a congress, or as a technical document of an institution. text
publication_topic The general topic of the publication, completely subjective. text
publication_excerpts Parts of the document that I have considered of interest, completely subjective. Most of the text is copied from the publications, and therefore referenced to the authors. text
publication_obs Pertinent observations about the publication. text
production_system Abbreviation of the production system in the experiment: ICLF = Integrated Crop-Livestock-Forestry; ICL = Integrated Crop-Livestock; ILF = Integrated Livestock-Forestry; C = Crop; L = Livestock; F = Forestry; EG = Extensive Grazing; SF = Seasonal Forest. nominal category
plot_id Unique experiment plot indentificator within the same publication (not unique across publications). index
plot_area The area of the experimental plot. hectar (ha)
total_area The total area of the experiment (all plots together). hectar (ha)
forest_component The species used in the production system that are part of the forestry component (woody species). This variable can be composed of one or more species, separated by comma. Species identified by scientific name. nominal category
crop_component The species used in the production system that are part of the crop component. This variable can be composed of one or more species, separated by comma. Species identified by scientific name. nominal category
pasture_component The species used in the production system that are part of the pasture component. This variable can be composed of one or more species, separated by comma. Species identified by scientific name. nominal category
other_component The species used in the production system that are not part of any of the components. This variable can be composed of one or more species, separated by comma. Species identified by scientific name. nominal category
livestock_component The species used in the production system that are part of livestock component. This variable can be composed of one or more species, separated by comma. Species identified by common name. nominal category
min_stocking_rate The minimum number of animals grazing in one hectare of pasture in the experiment or experimental plot. float
stocking_rate Number of animals grazing in one hectare of pasture in the experiment or experimental plot. float
max_stocking_rate The maximum number of animals grazing in one hectare of pasture in the experiment or experimental plot. float
integration_type Describe how the species are integrated in an experimental plot. Can be rotation, consortium or succession. One experimental plot can have different integration techniques, those are separated by a comma. nominal category
integration_obs Pertinent observations about the integration techniques used in an experimental plot. text
trees_spacing The spacing between trees adopted in an experimental plot. The spacing is represented by meters between trees. nominal category
trees_population The amount of trees individuals in one hectare of production. integer
soil_management Brief description of any relevant practice in soil management. text
previous_use What was the land use before the adoption of the production system analysed in the experiment. nominal category
establishment What was the year of the adoption of the production system analysed in the experiment. year according to the gregorian calendar
experiment_period Which years of the start and end of the experiment. The period is represented by two years, separated by a line. years according to the gregorian calendar
federative_unit The federative unit inside Brazil where the experimental plot was established. nominal category
municipality The municipality inside Brazil where the experimental plot was established. nominal category
experiment_location The specific location (e.g. farm name, institution unit) where the experimental plot was established. nominal category
coordinates The geographic coordinates provided to indicate the location of the experimental plot. text
soil_type_sibcs The soil type of the of the experimental plot(s), according to the Brazilian Soil Classification System. nominal category
soil_type_wrb The soil type of the of the experimental plot(s), according to the World Reference Base. nominal category
annual_precip Average historical accumulated precipitation in a year, for the experimental plot. milimeters (mm)
min_temp Average historical minimum temperature in a year, for the experimental plot. celsius degrees (ºC)
mean_temp Average historical mean temperature in a year, for the experimental plot. celsius degrees (ºC)
max_temp Average historical maximum temperature in a year, for the experimental plot. celsius degrees (ºC)
management What is the management practice being described. nominal category
product What is the product being applied. nominal category
formula What is the formula of the product being applied (e.g. what is the proportion of nutrients in the fertilizer). text
quantity The amount of product being applied. float
year What is the year that the observation was recorded. integer
soil_layer The layer of the soil, in depth, in which the observations were recorded. centimeters (cm)
clay_content The proportion of clay in the observations. grams per kilogram (g/kg)
silt_content The proportion of silt in the observations. grams per kilogram (g/kg)
sand_content The proportion of sand in the observations. grams per kilogram (g/kg)
bulk_density The soil bulk density in the observations. kilograms per cubic meter (kg/m³)
total_porosity The soil total porosity in the observations. percent (%)
particle_density The soil particle density in the observations. kilograms per cubic meter (kg/m³)
organic matter The amount of organic matter in the soil. grams per kilogram (g/kg)
organic_carbon_content The soil organic carbon content in the observations. grams per kilogram (g/kg)
correction If carbon content estimates were corrected. 0 = no correction, 1 = bulk density correction, 2 = bulk density and soil mass correction.
number_of_observations The amount of observations that were used to generate the record.
estimator What estimator was used to represent the obervations.
position The position of the observations in relation to the tree component (how far the observations were performed in relation to the trees lines). meters
measured If the observations were measured or estimated. 1 = measured, 0 = estimated integer
roots_fraction The fraction of roots composing the trees. percentage (%)
stem_fraction The fraction of stem composing the trees. percentage (%)
canopy_fraction The fraction of canopy composing the trees. percentage (%)
roots_volume The volume of roots composing the trees. cubic meters (m³)
stem_volume The volume of stem composing the trees. cubic meters (m³)
canopy_volume The volume of canopy composing the trees. cubic meters (m³)
roots_biomass The biomass of roots composing the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
stem_biomass The biomass of stem composing the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
canopy_biomass The biomass of canopy composing the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
roots_carbon The carbon of roots composing the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
stem_carbon The carbon of stem composing the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
canopy_carbon The carbon of canopy composing the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
total_biomass The biomass of the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
total_carbon The carbon of the trees. tons per hectare (t/ha)
diameter The diameter of the trees. meters per hectare (m/ha)
basal_area The basal area of the trees. squared meters per hectare (m²/ha)
height The height of the trees. meters (m)
mortality_rate The amount of trees that died. percentage (%)
year_after_planting The years after tree planting where the observations were registered.
month_after_planting The months after tree planting where the observations were registered.
specie What is the specie being observed.
population The amount of individuals planted per hectare. plants per hectare (n/ha)
yield The yield of the crop. kilograms per hectare (kg/ha)
spacing The spacing in relation to the crop component. meters (m)
sowing_rate The amount of seeds used in one hectare. kilograms per hectare (kg/ha)
leaf_yield The leaf dry matter yield of the pasture. kilograms per hectare (kg/ha)
dry_matter_accumulation The total dry matter accumulation of the pasture. kilograms per hectare per day (kg/ha day)
digestability The in vitro digestability of the pasture. percentage (%)
crude_protein The protein concentration of the pasture. percentage (%)
lai The leaf area index of the pasture squared meters per squared meters (m²/m²)
sla The specific leaf area of the pasture squared centimeters per gram (cm²/g)
grazing_method What was the grazing method used in the plot.
live_mean_weigth The live mean weight of the livestock. kilograms (kg)
average_daily_gain The weight gain of the livestock per day grams per animal per day (g/AU day)
carcass The ilograms of carcass produced per hectar. kilograms per hectare (kg/ha)
age The age of the livestock in months. month
month What is the month that the observation was recorded. integer
source What is the source of the emissions.
gas What is the gas being observed.
accumulated_emissions The ammount of emissions in a year, in one hectare. kilograms per hectare (kg/ha)
par_transmittance The amount of photossinthetically active radiation that is transmitted by the tree component canopy. percentage (%)
